Session 02 · Niche — Ideas

Niche Ideas Workbook

Built to generate, not decide. This session widens the field — we score and choose next time. You've got depth and plenty of ideas; this is where you point that at one group with one problem. A loose first pass is exactly right — and bring plenty. More candidates, less polish, is exactly what we want.

✓
Keep every idea in the coaching lane. A niche moves someone from well to better — designing a stronger future. It never treats a problem (that's therapy). Your clinical knowledge of a group is the raw material; the transformation you sell must be a coaching one.
▸ How to approach this

One relaxed sitting, about 50 minutes. The goal is a wide spread of loose, unpolished ideas — not a finished answer, and not every box filled.

Where your best ideas come from

  • The populations you already know from clinical work
  • What those groups said, again and again
  • The changes you'd genuinely love to deliver

As you go

  • Skim the three menus and the examples first
  • Add your own — the menus are inspiration, not limits
  • Don't judge or score — quantity now, choosing later

Two reminders

  • A loose first pass, and plenty of it
  • No wrong answers — we finish it together on the call
  • Every idea stays in the coaching lane
Step 01 · Generate

Transformations

The change you deliver — the "after" your client is actually buying.

Pick around three you could deliver passionately — pull from the menu or add your own. Passion matters; you'll be doing this a long time.

Step 02 · Generate

Audiences & Their Pain

The people you're best placed to help — especially those you already understand from clinical work.

Add the groups that pull at you. Jot any pain points that come to mind, in their words — we'll mine more together on the call.

Step 03 · Generate

Propositions

Turn the strong pairs into one line each — the first draft of your message.

Draft as many as come; five to eight is a great haul. Don't polish, don't score — we do that next session.

Step 04 · Session 3

Score & Select

Rate each proposition to find the strongest niche. Every score is 1–10; the highest total points the way.
